We Stand with Israel
Add an icon to your store header showing your support for Israel during this difficult time.
To add the extension to your Shopify store:
1. Open your Shopify theme editor.
2. Under the Header section, click Header.
3. Click Add block.
4. Under App Blocks, select We Stand with Israel. You can choose a classic colored icon or dark colored icon.
5. Click Save.
