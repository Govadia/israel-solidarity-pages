We Stand with Israel
Add an icon to your store header showing your support for Israel during this difficult time.
To add the icon using theme app extension for Online Store 2.0:
1. Open your Shopify theme editor.
2. Under the Header section, click Header.
3. Click Add block.
4. Under App Blocks, select We Stand with Israel. You can choose a classic colored icon or dark colored icon.
5. Click Save.
To add the icon using liquid (available for all):
1. Open your Shopify theme editor.
2. Click on theme menu on the top (3 dots) and click on Edit code.
3. Go to your header liquid file
4. Add the following snippet to your header liquid file: 
<div class="israel-solidarity-flag-container" onmouseover="this.style.transform='scale(1.1)'" onmouseout="this.style.transform='scale(1)'">
    <img src="https://we-stand-with-israel.s3.amazonaws.com/assets/israel-solidarity-flag.svg"
        class="israel-solidarity-flag-img"
        title="We stand united in support for Israel, and condemn the acts of terrorism that have brought devastation and claimed innocent lives. We hope for stability and safety to be restored, hostages to be safely reunited with their loved ones, and lost lives be properly honored"
        alt="We stand with Israel"
        height="32"
        width="32"
        loading="eager">
</div>

5. Click Save.
